# Slack integration

Slack has become a popular communication tool that brings Managers, DevOps and Engineering team members together in their daily tasks. 
You can connect your Slack account with CloudHub to access CloudHub’s functionality [directly from Slack](https://docs.cloudhub.com/integrations.html#slack-app).

To prepare your CloudHub cluster for Slack integration, follow these instructions: [CloudHub/slacker/README.md](../slacker/README.md)
